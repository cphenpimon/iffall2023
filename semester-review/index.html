Interaction Foundations – Fall 2023

Mango Sticky Rice Recipe Page

This recipe site features my favorite dish—Mango Sticky Rice—with a focus on a clean and simple presentation. The page is designed for easy navigation, providing both beginners and experienced cooks with a straightforward experience.

Hilma af Klint Microsite - Creative Director

Collaborating with Jeffery Gong, I took on the role of creative director for a microsite dedicated to one of my favorite artists, Hilma af Klint. The primary goal was to create a site that would showcase her art with utmost reverance. Opting for a neutral color palette and a generally minimalistic style, the intention was for the artwork to speak for itself. Jeffrey did a great job at bringing my vision to life and I commend his work on the interactive gallery page.

Time Microsite - Designer/Developer

Leah Fontenot was an amazing creative director to work under to develop this site. Initially, the project's scope felt overwhelming due to its all-encompassing nature. Together, we successfully crafted a classic and modern black-and-white aesthetic that exudes sophistication and timelessness. The reponsive design was the most challenging aspect for me but I'm very proud of the end result.
