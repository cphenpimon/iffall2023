the perfect pair

beabadoobee

Album: Beatopia

Release Date: July 15, 2022

An experimental bossa-nova song, "the perfect pair" describes communication issues between a couple. Many of the lyrics detail the silence, or stonewalling, from Bea's partner, something she comes to realize she does herself.
